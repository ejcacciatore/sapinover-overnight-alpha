Research Disclaimer

This analysis constitutes independent research on market microstructure and execution timing patterns. Timing differential metrics represent geometric positioning within overnight price intervals: Timing Differential = (Pₜ - Pᵥ) / Pₐ × 10,000 bps where Pₐ = prior close (anchor), Pₜ = next open (terminal), Pᵥ = overnight VWAP. This is not investment advice. Past observations do not predict future outcomes. Sapinover LLC conducts independent market microstructure research.

Data Sources: Trade data collected daily from venue operations. Market data sourced from publicly available APIs. Future releases will incorporate additional venue data from alternative trading systems.
